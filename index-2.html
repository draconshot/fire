Chocolate-Cheesecake Cookies

Created by Sebastian Le

1/7/19

Cheescake and cookies combine to make something delicious and easy to make.

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packled brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teapspoon baking powder
  • 1 teapsoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cups chopped pecans

Fillings

  • 8 ounce paclkage cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl using an electric mixer until smooth. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract until creamy.
  2. Sift flour, cocoa powder, and baking soda together in a bowl. Add flour mixture to butter mixture. Fold in nuts. Refrigerate dough for 1 hour.
  3. While dough is chilling, beat cream cheese, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until smooth.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. Remove dough from fridge and form about 1 tablespoon dough into a ball; make an indentation and fill with about 1 teaspoon filling. Place cookie on an ungreased baking sheet. Repeat with remaining dough and filling.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, 11 to 13 minutes. Allow cookies to cool completely before removing from the baking sheet, about 20 minutes.
